China

Skyscrapers, delicious food, and mesmerizing temples oh my!

Ni Hao!

China was my biggest surprise and delight of 2017. I ignorantly expected to feel smothered by the crowds of people and pollution. Instead, I was pleasantly impressed everyday with the scale of their tremendous architectural feats, their welcoming and friendly people, and their spectacularly diverse and delicious food. As an aspiring business professional, I can expect to work more and more with Chinese partners and to learn about their culture and impressive country first hand was an invaludable experience.
