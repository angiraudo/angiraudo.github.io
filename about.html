Meet Andrea

MBA Student, E-Commerce Geek, and Wannabe Professional Wanderluster

Hi! Hola!

My name is Andrea Giraudo and I'm a 2nd year MBA student at the Kellogg School of Management. I carry three passports (Argentina, Italy, & U.S.A.) so basically I think I'm Jason Bourne and I just love to travel. My love for travel started from a very young age as it was a custom in my house that instead of receiving Christmas gifts, my brother and I were taken on a trip somewhere during the holidays. Welcome to my travel diary where I share a bit of memories and photographs from my travels around the world during my business school experience. Each week I'll add new countries to every region of the world. Soon to come are my tips and tricks for expert travels and even some yummy recipes from my travels!
